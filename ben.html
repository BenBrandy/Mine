My First WebPage as a learner.

I begun my web development practices from scratch with this site. This is the link to w3schools where I get the knowledge to build this skeleton webpage w3schools. This site offers step by step and topicwise exhaustive lessons for the hotcake programming languages.

It all started with a few intro lessons from ex-Moringa software engineering student, Wycliffe Orao at UoN Lower Kabete Campus, the lessons came to a halt when the first case of COVID-19 was reported in Kenya in May 2020. During lockdown I decided to shift to YouTube videos where I started polishing the little knowledge from Orao. I lost my machine due to motherboard failure and had to pause everything. I am currently resuming from ground zero and catching up.
